About Us!

'Dine @ #AnVen' is located in Coastal Road,Flic en Flac situated in the South-West of Mauritius.
The restaurant offers a nice atmosphere to go with family and friends with a tropical and contemporary themed decor.
As Mauritius is a small island with a presence of several cultures.
'Dine @ #AnVen' believes in sharing their culture by nourishing its customers
by serving tropical Mauritian and Indian cuisine available in our four branches around the island.
